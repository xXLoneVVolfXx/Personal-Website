LoneVVolf Studios is an individual-owned group. Founded by LoneVVolf, also known as Estevan Lerma, the group was created back in 2017. Everything first began in Roblox, a free website with games that also allows you to create games for free. Being intrigued with Lua coding, the main language to code games in Roblox, LoneVVolf began to create whatever fascinating ideas came to thought. From building a tree to a skyscraper. This is where his coding career began. Soon after, he founded LoneVVolf studios to generate a source of income from his creations.
